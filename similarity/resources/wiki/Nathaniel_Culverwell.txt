He was baptized on 14 January 1619 at the church of St. Margaret Moses where his father was rector. He was the second of six children of Richard and Margaret (Horton) Culverwell.[1]

A student (admitted 1633) and later a fellow of Emmanuel College, Cambridge,[2] he was associated with members of the Cambridge Platonists group.
