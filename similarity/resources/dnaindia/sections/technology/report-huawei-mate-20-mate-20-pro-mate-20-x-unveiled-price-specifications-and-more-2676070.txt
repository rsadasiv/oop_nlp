At an event held in London, Huawei launched three smartphones - Huawei Mate 20, Huawei Mate 20 Pro and Huawei Mate 20 X.
The Huawei Mate 20 for the 4GB RAM + 64GB internal storage variant is priced at €799 (approximately Rs 67,850) while the 6GB RAM + 128GB internal storage variant is priced at €849 (approximately Rs 72,000). The Huawei Mate 20 Pro for the 6GB RAM + 128GB internal storage variant is priced at €1,049 (approximately Rs 89,000) while the Huawei Mate 20 X for the 6GB RAM + 128GB internal storage variant is priced at €899 (approximately Rs 76,350). The Porsche Design Huawei Mate 20 RS for the 4GB RAM + 128GB internal storage variant is priced at €1,695 (approximately Rs 1,43,900) and the 6GB RAM + 128GB internal storage variant is priced at €2,095 (approximately Rs 1,77,900)
The Huawei Watch GT for the Sport variant is priced at €199 (approximately Rs 16,900), Classic is priced at €249 (approximately Rs 21,150) and the Huawei Band 3 Pro is priced at €99 (approximately Rs 8,400).
Huawei Mate 20 
In terms of specifications, the device features a 6.53-inch full-HD+ display with an aspect ratio of 18:7:9 and 1080x2244 pixels pixels resolution. It is powered by a HiSilicon Kirin 980 7nm SoC paired with 4GB RAM and 6 RAM options. Running Android 9.0 Pie, the device sports triple rear camera set up – 16MP sensor with ultra-wide-angle lens (f/2.2), a 12MP sensor with wide-angle lens (f/2.4) and an 8MP sensor. Connectivity features include Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n/ac, Bluetooth 4.2 and microUSB. A 4000mAh battery completes the package.
Huawei Mate 20 Pro 
In terms of specifications, the device features a 6.39-inch QHD+ display with an aspect ratio of 19:5:9 and 1440x3120 pixels resolution. It is powered by a HiSilicon Kirin 980 7nm SoC paired with 6GB of RAM. Running Android 9.0 Pie, the device includes a triple rear camera set up – 40MP primary sensor with wide-angle lens (f/1.8), a 20MP sensor with ultra-wide-angle lens (f/2.2), and the third is an 8MP sensor. It also has a 24MP front camera. Connectivity features include 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth v5.0, GPS/ A-GPS, and a USB Type-C port.
Huawei Mate 20 X
In terms of specifications, the device features a 7.2-inch AMOLED display. It is powered by a HiSilicon Kirin 980 processor paired with 6GB RAM. It also includes an internal storage of 128GB of storage. A 5000mAh battery completes the package.
