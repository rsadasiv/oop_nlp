'Bade Achche Lagte Hain' star Sakshi Tanwar, who was last seen in Aamir Khan starrer 'Dangal', adopted a baby girl this Navratri. The actor has named her Dityaa, which is another name of Goddess Lakshmi. 
Sharing her happiness, Sakshi told Times of India, “With the blessings of my parents and the support of my family and friends, I have adopted a baby girl who would soon turn 9 months old. I am extremely delighted to share my happiness with you as I welcome the bundle of joy in my life. This is undoubtedly the greatest moment of my life and I and my entire family are elated to embrace Dityaa. She is the answer to all my prayers and I feel blessed to have her in my life.”
Sakshi Tanwar is one of the most reverred actors of Indian television. With shows such as 'Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii', 'Bade Achhe Lagte Hai', 'Devi' and others, she is one of the most loved and respected actors. She played Aamir Khan’s wife in 'Dangal' and has an unreleased film with Sunny Deol, 'Mohalla Assi'.
