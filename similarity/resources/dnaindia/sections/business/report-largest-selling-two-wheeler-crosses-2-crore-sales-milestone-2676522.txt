Honda Motorcycle & Scooter India’s (HMSI) flagship Activa brand of automatic scooters has crossed 2 crore-customer-mark. Activa pushed Hero MotorCorp’s flagship Splendor to second position in May 2017
2001 – Year of launch
55,000 – Units sold in the debut year
15 yrs – It took to reach 1 crore customers
3 yrs – HMSI took for next 1 crore customers
1.2mn – Annual capacity at HMSI’s Vithalapur unit in Gujarat, the world’s largest scooter-only plant
In 2012, the volume had breached the 5-million mark
Over 33% – HMSI contributes to global volume for the parent Honda
*It is the largest volume contributor for the Japanese company
