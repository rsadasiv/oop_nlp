Fashion’s ‘more is more’ philosophy isn’t taking a backseat given some of the breathtaking looks at the London Fashion Week. Designers narrated their runway story in their Spring Summer 19 outings with the transformative power of exuberant ruffles which swooshed and rustled as models strutted down the runways.
Richard Quinn — the biggest talking point of this season expressed his love for the artisanal florals by presenting them in tiered frothy ruffled creations. Ruffles were also the mainstay at Molly Goddard, Delpozo and Preen with designers scaling them to dramatic effects.
