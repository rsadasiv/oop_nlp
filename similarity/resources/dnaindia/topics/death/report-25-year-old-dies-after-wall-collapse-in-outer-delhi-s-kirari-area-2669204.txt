A 25-year-old man died after the wall of an under construction building, where he was working, collapsed in outer Delhi's Kirari area on Thursday morning. A case has been registered and further investigation under way, police said.
According to the police, the deceased has been identified as Chotu Kumar (25) who was working at the under construction house of Praveen.
"Kumar was working on the second floor of the building when the roof collapsed. He along with the roof fell on the floor," said a senior officer.
Police said that locals rushed him to a nearby hospital where he was declared brought dead on arrival. After the post-mortem the police handed over the body to the family members.
"We have registered a case under section 288 (Negligent conduct with respect to pulling down or repairing buildings) and 304A (Causing death by negligence) of the Indian Penal Code and booked the owner of the house Praveen. However, no arrest has been made so far," said a senior police officer.
