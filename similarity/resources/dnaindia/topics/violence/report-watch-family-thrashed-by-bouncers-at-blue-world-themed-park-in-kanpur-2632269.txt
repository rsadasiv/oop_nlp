On Monday, a family was threatened and abused by bouncers at a water park in Kanpur on Sunday. The incident reported happened after the family was denied permission to get on a ride in the park. The incident was reported from the Blue World Theme Park in Kanpur. 
News agency ANI reported: "“Bouncers at Blue World Theme Water Park thrashed and abused a family after an argument over permission to use a ride in the park."
