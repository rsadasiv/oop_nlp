Stephanie Clare Brookes (born 20 December 1980 in Stoke-on-Trent) is a food writer and presenter. She has a regular segment on BBC Radio London, where she discusses the latest street food, restaurants and eateries, and the newest food markets.

Brookes is the food contributor on the JoAnne Good and Simon Lederman Saturday morning show. There she discusses the street food around London, as well as restaurants and eateries throughout the city.

Brookes had previously written in the Lifestyle/Mind Body Spirit genre. While in her twenties, she had a regular online column in My Bliss magazine, which led to her first mainstream book, How to Be a Spiritual Goddess (Piatkus). Her following book, Meditation Made Easy (CICO Books) was later relaunched as The Little Pocket Book of Meditation.
